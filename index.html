How to play

  • Paddle with ←/→ or A/D.
  • Press the blue button to answer a question. Correct launches one ball (+50 points).
  • +10 per brick. Clear all bricks to advance a level; speed increases and the paddle narrows.
  • Pause with P. Mute with M. Enter submits in the question dialog; Esc closes it.

Accessibility: Semantic landmarks, visible focus, keyboard support, and polite live announcements. No time limits while the question dialog is open.
